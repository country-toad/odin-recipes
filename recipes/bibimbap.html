Bibimbap

Description

Trust the Koreans to transform the humble rice bowl into a recipe that’s revered all around the world! With a kaleidoscope of seasoned sautéed vegetables, Korean marinated beef, and the signature fried egg, the thing that really seals the deal is the bright red, spicy Bibimbap Sauce that I can’t get enough of.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Korean BBQ Sauce
  • Cucumber
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Chop onion.
  2. Stir fry chopped onion, garlic, and olive oil for 5 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and sauce. Cook for another 5 min. Add everything to bowl.
  4. Chop bell peppers and cook in pan for 5 minutes. Add spinach and cook for another 3 min. Add to bowl.
  5. Chop cucumber and add to bowl. You're done!
